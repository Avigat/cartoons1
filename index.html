Tom & Jerry

Tom and Jerry is an American animated franchise and series of comedy short films. The series centers on the rivalry between the title characters Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse.

Doraemon

Doraemon is a Japanese manga series which revolves around an robotic cat named Doraemon, who travels back in time from the 22nd century to help a boy named Nobita Nobi.

Shin Chan

Shin Chan is again a Japanese manga series and it follows the comedy adventures of a boy named "Shin" Nohara and his family.
